About the group

This group was composed of 6 Grade 10 STEM Students studying in Koronadal National Comprehensive High School (KNCHS) in Koronadal City, South Cotabato. The group was being headed by Jorge Sabido (16 years old) (the very left). The other team members are the following (from left to right): Christine Mayo (16 years old), Kristine Espera (15 years old), Joever Rafael (16 years old), John Bernal (15 years old), and Haya Mamao (15 years old) . They joined the NASA Space Apps Challenge and currently on competition.
